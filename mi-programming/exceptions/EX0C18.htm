0C18

Date Boundary Underflow

The end result of a date calculation or conversion of the date precedes the beginning of the time line described in the DDAT.

Instructions Causing Exception:

  • Compute Date Duration (CDD)
  • Compute Timestamp Duration (CTSD)
  • Convert Date (CVTD)
  • Convert Timestamp (CVTTS)
  • Decrement Date (DECD)
  • Decrement Timestamp (DECTS)
  • Increment Date (INCD)
  • Increment Timestamp (INCTS)
