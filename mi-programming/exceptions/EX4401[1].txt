<TABLE CELLPADDING="3">
<TR VALIGN="TOP"><TD><P><B>4401
</B></TD><TD><P><I>Object Domain or Hardware Storage Protection Violation
</I>
<P>
An attempt was made to use a blocked instruction or to access
protected storage.
Protected storage is
in the system domain or
has storage protection attributes that restrict its use.
Only programs running with system state can use blocked instructions.
<P>
The execution of the instruction is suppressed when the exception is
signalled.
<font color=blue>
<P><B>Note: </B>Below, IPS stands for "implicit process space".
